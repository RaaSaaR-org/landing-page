# Claude Code Instructions for EmAI

@README.md

## Project Overview
This is the EmAI (Embodied AI) landing page. EmAI helps companies understand and use cognitive robots powered by open-source AI. We bring robots into real environments, collect real-world data on what works, and share that knowledge through testing, workshops, and consulting.

## Key Principles
- **Professional and industrial**: Content should be knowledge-oriented for enterprise customers
- **Human-first**: Robots assist humans and fill gaps -- they do NOT replace workers
- **Use "kognitive Roboter"**: Never use "humanoide Roboter"
- **Dark theme only**: All UI uses dark backgrounds with orange (#FF6700) accents
- **Bilingual**: German is primary language, English for international reach
- **Knowledge-first**: Lead with what we know and share, not what we sell

## Target Audience
1. **Manufacturing companies**: Evaluating cognitive robotics for their operations
2. **Logistics operations**: Understanding automation potential
3. **Mid-sized companies (Mittelstand)**: Making robotics accessible beyond large corporations
4. **Research organizations**: Collaborating on real-world testing and data

## Brand Guidelines
- **Primary Color**: #FF6700 (Caltrans Orange)
- **Secondary Color**: #2DD4BF (Teal)
- **Background**: #141414 (Base dark)
- **Surface**: #1F1F1F (Cards, modals)
- **Text Primary**: #F5F5F4
- **Text Secondary**: #A8A29E

## Content Guidelines
- Focus on German/European market
- Key services: Real-world testing, data collection, workshops & training, consulting
- Key messages: Learn by doing, open-source AI, European sovereignty, no vendor lock-in
- EmAI is an Embodied AI consultancy (not a "platform" or "RaaS company")
- RaaS is one business case among others -- not the primary messaging
- Emphasize: Independent, European, Practice-oriented
- Maintain professional tone -- industrial but approachable
- Human-centric messaging -- robots fill gaps, not replace people
