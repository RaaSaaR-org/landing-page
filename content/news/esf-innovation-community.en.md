We're glad to share that the board of the EastSideFab Innovation Community has **unanimously** approved EmAI Robotics GmbH's membership by circular resolution. As of **1 June 2026**, we are an official member.

![Member of the EastSideFab Innovation Community](/badges/esf-mitglied-weiss.svg)

## What is EastSideFab?

[EastSideFab](https://eastsidefab.de) is an innovation community where companies, researchers, and practitioners come together not just to discuss new technologies, but to try them out. That approach fits us well: we learn by doing, collect data from real-world deployments, and share what works -- and what doesn't.

## Why it's a good fit

Cognitive robotics doesn't mature in the lab alone -- it matures where people, machines, and real tasks meet. A network like EastSideFab gives us:

- **Proximity to practice**: Exchange with companies facing concrete automation questions.
- **Collaboration**: An environment where joint tests and open knowledge transfer are the norm.
- **Regional roots**: Innovation grounded in the region, in line with our principles -- independent, European, practice-oriented.

## What we want to contribute

We bring what we do best: getting cognitive robots into real environments, assessing them vendor-neutrally, and making the resulting knowledge accessible. We look forward to joint projects, honest discussions, and robots that support people -- not replace them.

If you're part of EastSideFab or considering a collaboration, [write to us](mailto:info@EmAI.dev). We answer personally.
