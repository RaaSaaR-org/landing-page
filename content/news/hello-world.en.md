Welcome to the EmAI news section. From now on, we share what's happening here: new real-world tests, events, workshops, projects we've won, publications.

## Why a dedicated news section?

If you're interested in cognitive robotics, you often want more than theory -- you want to know: what are they actually doing right now? Which robots are running at which customer? What did they learn this week?

We want to address those questions honestly here -- without PR language, without a sales pitch.

## What you can expect

- **Events**: Where we're showing up, what talks we're giving.
- **Practice reports**: What we've learned from recent tests.
- **Publications**: Whitepapers, data, open-source contributions.
- **Team & growth**: New colleagues, new locations, new partners.
